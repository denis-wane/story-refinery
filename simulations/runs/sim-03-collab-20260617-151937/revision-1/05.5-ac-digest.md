## Root Cause Assessment

The AC Writer is being asked to generate AC for 10 stories in a single call. At the observed 44KB prompt size and typical AC verbosity (10–30 Given/When/Then blocks per story), the expected output is likely 8,000–20,000 tokens — well beyond what a 180s timeout will accommodate.

**Recommended fix:** The AC Writer should process **one story at a time**, not all stories in a batch. The pipeline should loop the AC Writer step per story and concatenate results. This would bring each call to ~1,500–3,000 output tokens and easily clear the 180s threshold.
