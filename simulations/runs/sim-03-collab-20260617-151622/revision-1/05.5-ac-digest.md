## Recommended Action

The pipeline cannot proceed to Quality Review. Two likely root causes:

1. **Single-call scope too large.** The AC Writer is being asked to generate AC for all 10 stories in one call. Split into per-story calls (one invocation per feature slice from the decomposition).
2. **Timeout too short for output volume.** If AC must be generated in one call, 180s is insufficient. Either increase the timeout ceiling or reduce the per-story AC depth in the prompt (e.g., cap at 5 AC per story in the initial pass).

**Recommended fix:** Invoke the AC Writer once per story (10 sequential or parallel calls), collect outputs, then pass the combined result to this summarizer.
